It isn't everyday
that you meet a bunch of stellar people
who you can look upto
and who guide you through the twists and turns of college life,
but all good things must come to an end
and it is time for us to bid you farewell.
It now is our turn to nudge you out of your nest
as you fly into your own colorful futures
Join us as we party for the last time
(officially that is) and as we shed tears of reminiscence and parting.

The Climax 2017
